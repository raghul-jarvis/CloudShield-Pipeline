Frequently Asked Questions

Are sessions confidential? ▼
Absolutely. All sessions are completely private and confidential. Your personal information and what you share will never be disclosed to anyone without your explicit consent.
How do online sessions work? ▼
After booking, you'll receive confirmation via WhatsApp or email. Sessions are conducted over voice call or chat — whichever you prefer. All you need is your phone and a quiet, private space.
Can I reschedule my session? ▼
Yes! Life happens. You can reschedule your session by contacting us on WhatsApp at least 24 hours before your scheduled time, and we'll find a new slot that works for you.
What happens after 8 sessions? ▼
After completing 8 sessions, you can continue with additional sessions at standard per-session charges. Many clients choose to continue as needed for ongoing support and growth.
